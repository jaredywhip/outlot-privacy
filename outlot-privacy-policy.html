2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: information you provide directly, information collected automatically when you use the App, and information generated by our AI systems on your behalf.

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you register as a professional estate sale company, we may also collect your business name, business address, and phone number.

Item Photos. When you use the App to photograph estate sale items, those images are captured through your device's camera and uploaded to our servers. These photos may contain images of personal property belonging to the estate.

Voice Notes. When you record voice notes describing items, those audio recordings are captured through your device's microphone and transmitted to our servers for processing. Voice notes typically contain your verbal description of item condition, provenance, and other details.

Sale Information. You may provide sale addresses, dates, item details, pricing decisions, and other information related to managing estate sales through the App.

Feedback and Communications. If you contact us for support, participate in beta testing, or provide feedback, we collect the content of those communications.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information. We collect information about the device you use to access the App, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.

Usage Data. We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, screens visited, actions taken, and the dates and times of your activity.

Log Data. Our servers automatically record information when you use the Service, including your IP address, browser type, and referring/exit pages.

We do not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting services. If we add these in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect any additional data collection.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Precise Location. The App does not request access to your device's GPS or precise location. Sale addresses are entered manually by you.

Contacts, Calendars, or Health Data. We do not access your contacts, calendar, health data, or other sensitive device data.

Children's Data. The Service is designed for professional estate sale companies and is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

4. Third-Party AI Processing — OpenAI

This section is important. Please read it carefully.

Outlot uses artificial intelligence to generate inventory listings from your photos and voice notes. To power this functionality, we transmit certain data to OpenAI, L.L.C. ("OpenAI"), a third-party AI service provider. Here is exactly what that means:

4.1 What Data Is Sent to OpenAI

When you use the AI listing feature, the following data is transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing:

  • Item photos you capture or upload, which are sent to OpenAI's vision model (GPT-4o) to identify items, assess condition, and generate descriptions.
  • Voice note transcriptions, which are processed through OpenAI's Whisper model to convert your spoken descriptions into text. The audio is transmitted to OpenAI for transcription.

We do not send OpenAI your name, email address, account credentials, business information, or any personal information beyond the item photos and voice recordings you submit for AI processing.

4.2 How OpenAI Handles Your Data

We access OpenAI's services through their business API, which operates under the following terms:

  • OpenAI does not train its AI models on data submitted through the API. Your photos and voice notes are not used to improve OpenAI's models.
  • Data retention: OpenAI retains API inputs and outputs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring and safety purposes, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Processing location: OpenAI processes data in the United States.

For more information, see OpenAI's Enterprise Privacy page and their Data Processing Addendum.

4.3 Your Consent

The first time you use an AI-powered feature in the App, you will be presented with a clear explanation of this data sharing and asked to provide your explicit consent before any data is transmitted to OpenAI. You may decline, in which case AI features will not be available, but you may continue to use other features of the App.

You can withdraw your consent at any time in the App's settings, which will disable AI-powered features going forward. Withdrawing consent does not affect data already processed.

4.4 Photos May Contain Sensitive Content

Estate sale item photos may incidentally capture personal property, family photographs, documents, or other items belonging to the estate. While our system is designed to focus on the items being inventoried, we cannot guarantee that incidental personal information visible in photos will not be processed by OpenAI. We recommend avoiding photographing sensitive personal documents or information that is not relevant to the inventory listing.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account information: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Item photos and voice recordings: Retained for as long as the associated sale or listing exists in your account, or until you delete them.
  • AI-generated listings: Retained for as long as the associated sale exists in your account, or until you delete them.
  • Usage data and logs: Retained for up to 12 months for operational and security purposes.
  • OpenAI processing: OpenAI retains API data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring (see Section 4.2).

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
